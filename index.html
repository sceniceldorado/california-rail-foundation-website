About Us

Forest Lawn Cemetery is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization dedicated to preserving the dignity, heritage, and memory of our community. Established to serve families with compassion and respect, we provide a serene resting place and maintain the grounds with care. Our nonprofit status ensures that all donations directly support preservation, maintenance, and community remembrance programs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to honor lives, preserve history, and provide a peaceful environment for reflection. We believe cemeteries are sacred spaces that connect generations, offering comfort and continuity. Through stewardship and community engagement, we ensure Forest Lawn Cemetery remains a place of dignity and legacy.

Programs & Initiatives

  • Grounds Preservation: Maintaining landscapes, monuments, and memorials with care.
  • Community Engagement: Hosting remembrance events and educational programs.
  • Historical Preservation: Documenting and protecting heritage for future generations.

Donate

As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, your contributions are tax-deductible. Donations help us maintain the cemetery, support community programs, and preserve history. Every gift strengthens our mission.
