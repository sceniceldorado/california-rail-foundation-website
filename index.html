Preserving California's Rail Heritage

The California Rail Foundation supports rail preservation, education programs, and sustainable transportation initiatives across California.

About the Foundation

The California Rail Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to preserving rail history, supporting transportation infrastructure, and educating communities about the environmental and economic benefits of rail transportation.

Through educational programs, partnerships, and preservation projects, the foundation works to promote awareness and support for rail transportation systems.

Nonprofit Status
