About FOREST LAWN CEMETERY

FOREST LAWN CEMETERY is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization dedicated to providing compassionate, respectful, and affordable burial and memorial services to families in Bolivia, North Carolina, and surrounding areas. Our team ensures well-maintained grounds, serene surroundings, and support to families during difficult times. Established with the goal of preserving dignity and heritage, FOREST LAWN CEMETERY has become a trusted name in the community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a peaceful, beautiful, and well-maintained final resting place while supporting the families we serve with compassion and respect. As a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, we rely on community support and donations to continue our work and enhance our grounds, programs, and services.

Services We Provide

FOREST LAWN CEMETERY offers a wide range of services, including:

  • Traditional burial services
  • Cremation and memorial services
  • Monument and gravestone placement
  • Grounds maintenance and beautification
  • Support for families in planning memorials
