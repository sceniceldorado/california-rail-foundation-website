About Our Organization

The California Rail Foundation is a nonprofit organization committed to supporting rail transportation education, historical preservation, and community awareness about sustainable infrastructure. Rail transportation has played a significant role in the economic development and connectivity of California and continues to be an important part of modern transportation systems.


Our organization promotes educational initiatives that help communities understand the environmental and economic benefits of rail systems. We also support projects that preserve historic rail equipment, rail corridors, and transportation archives that document the history of rail development in California.


By partnering with community organizations, educators, and transportation advocates, the California Rail Foundation works to ensure that knowledge about rail transportation and its public benefits remains accessible to future generations.


The California Rail Foundation is recognized as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization in the United States. Donations may be tax-deductible as allowed by law.

Our Mission

The mission of the California Rail Foundation is to support the preservation and advancement of rail transportation through public education, historical documentation, and community outreach.


We strive to increase awareness about the role of rail infrastructure in sustainable transportation planning while preserving important historical materials related to the development of rail systems across California.

Programs and Activities

Rail Education Programs

Educational resources, public information materials, and community outreach initiatives designed to increase awareness about rail transportation and infrastructure.

Rail Preservation Efforts

Supporting projects that preserve historic rail equipment, archives, photographs, and documentation related to rail transportation history.

Community Engagement

Collaborating with local organizations and educational institutions to encourage public understanding of transportation infrastructure and sustainability.
