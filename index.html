About FOREST LAWN CEMETERY

FOREST LAWN CEMETERY is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to preserving the historical and cultural heritage of Bolivia, NC. We provide compassionate care to families seeking memorial services, maintaining serene grounds for reflection and remembrance. Our mission is to honor the lives of loved ones while serving the community with integrity and respect.

We are fully compliant with nonprofit regulations and proudly operate as a tax-exempt organization under the IRS.

Our Services

Burial Services

Comprehensive burial options with professional support to ensure every service meets your family's needs.

Memorial Services

Personalized memorials and tributes to celebrate the life of your loved ones.

Historical Preservation

Maintaining and documenting the history of our cemetery to honor generations past and future.

Our History

FOREST LAWN CEMETERY was founded decades ago to serve the Bolivia, NC community. Over the years, we have grown into a respected nonprofit organization dedicated to maintaining a tranquil, safe, and welcoming environment. Our volunteers and staff work tirelessly to preserve historical records, restore monuments, and ensure that our cemetery remains a sacred place for reflection and remembrance.
