About Us

The California Rail Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting the preservation, development, and education of rail transportation throughout California. Railroads have played a major role in the history, economic development, and infrastructure of the state.


Our organization works to promote awareness of the importance of rail systems, support historic rail preservation projects, and encourage sustainable transportation solutions for communities.


Through partnerships with community organizations, transportation advocates, and educational institutions, the California Rail Foundation aims to promote the benefits of rail transportation for future generations.


The California Rail Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Donations may be tax-deductible where permitted by law.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote the preservation and advancement of rail transportation through education, community engagement, and support for historical rail infrastructure.


The California Rail Foundation works to highlight the environmental, economic, and cultural importance of rail transportation. By supporting educational initiatives, historical preservation, and community programs, we strive to strengthen the role of rail systems in sustainable transportation planning.

Our Programs

Rail Education

Educational workshops and resources designed to teach communities about rail transportation, infrastructure, and sustainability.

Rail Preservation

Supporting projects that preserve historic railways, equipment, archives, and railway heritage across California.

Community Initiatives

Collaborating with communities and organizations to promote rail transportation development and public awareness.
