About Our Organization

The California Rail Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting rail transportation education, historical preservation, and public awareness about sustainable infrastructure.

Railroads have played a vital role in the development of California’s economy and communities. The California Rail Foundation works to ensure that knowledge of rail transportation and its historical significance continues to be preserved and shared with future generations.

The organization promotes educational outreach, preservation of rail history, and awareness of sustainable transportation systems.

The California Rail Foundation is a recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Donations may be tax-deductible where permitted by law.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote awareness, preservation, and education related to rail transportation infrastructure and history.

The California Rail Foundation works to support educational initiatives, historical preservation projects, and community engagement related to rail transportation systems and sustainable mobility.
