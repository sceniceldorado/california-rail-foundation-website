Our Mission

Forest Lawn Cemetery is dedicated to preserving dignity, heritage, and community memory. As a nonprofit 501(c)(3), our mission is to provide a serene resting place for loved ones, maintain the grounds with care, and honor the legacy of those who came before us. We believe cemeteries are sacred spaces that connect generations and foster reflection, respect, and remembrance.

Our History

Founded to serve the community of Bolivia, North Carolina, Forest Lawn Cemetery has been a cornerstone of local heritage for decades. Families have entrusted us with the care of their loved ones, knowing that we uphold traditions of respect and compassion. Our nonprofit status ensures that every dollar goes directly into maintaining the grounds, improving services, and supporting families in need.

Our Services

  • Grave site preparation and perpetual care
  • Memorial services and family support
  • Community remembrance events
  • Heritage preservation and historical documentation
  • Volunteer opportunities for groundskeeping and community outreach

Support Our Work

Our Policies

Nonprofit Transparency

Forest Lawn Cemetery operates under IRS 501(c)(3) regulations. We are committed to transparency, accountability, and ethical stewardship of all donations and resources.

Grounds Maintenance

We ensure perpetual care of all grave sites. Our staff and volunteers work diligently to maintain a peaceful and respectful environment.

Community Engagement

We host remembrance events, educational programs, and volunteer opportunities to strengthen community bonds and honor local heritage.

Privacy Policy

We respect the privacy of all families and donors. Personal information is never shared or sold, and is used solely for nonprofit operations.
