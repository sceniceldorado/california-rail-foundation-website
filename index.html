About SCENIC EL DORADO INC

SCENIC EL DORADO INC is a community-focused nonprofit organization committed to protecting the scenic beauty, natural landscapes, and environmental heritage of El Dorado, Kansas and surrounding communities. Through partnerships, volunteer initiatives, and public education, the organization works to preserve local environments and promote responsible stewardship of natural resources.

Our organization believes that healthy ecosystems and vibrant communities go hand in hand. We collaborate with local residents, schools, civic organizations, and businesses to ensure that future generations can enjoy the natural beauty and environmental health of our region.

SCENIC EL DORADO INC is recognized as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization under United States federal law. Donations made to our organization may be tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

Our Mission

Environmental Preservation

We work to preserve scenic landscapes, parks, and green spaces in the El Dorado area. By promoting conservation and responsible land stewardship, we help maintain natural ecosystems and biodiversity.

Community Engagement

SCENIC EL DORADO INC encourages residents to participate in environmental stewardship through volunteer programs, community projects, and public events that strengthen local pride and environmental responsibility.

Sustainable Development

We advocate for responsible development practices that balance economic growth with environmental sustainability, ensuring that progress does not come at the expense of natural beauty.

Programs & Initiatives

Community Beautification Projects

Our volunteers organize clean-up events, tree planting activities, and public space improvement initiatives that enhance the appearance and environmental health of local neighborhoods.

Environmental Education

SCENIC EL DORADO INC collaborates with schools and community groups to educate young people and residents about conservation, environmental protection, and sustainable living practices.

Public Awareness Campaigns

We promote awareness about environmental issues and encourage community members to take part in preserving local scenic resources.

Volunteer Opportunities

Volunteers are the heart of SCENIC EL DORADO INC. By joining our volunteer programs, individuals can directly contribute to community improvement and environmental protection.

Volunteer opportunities include:

  • Community clean-up events
  • Tree planting initiatives
  • Educational outreach
  • Event organization
  • Environmental monitoring projects
